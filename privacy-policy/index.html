Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

1. Introduction

Terra Connect Ltd (“we”, “us”, “our”, “Company”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website terraconnect.co.uk or engage with our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact information – name, email address, phone number (when you submit a form or contact us)
  • Device information – IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Usage data – pages visited, time spent on pages, referring URLs
  • Cookie data – see our Cookie Policy for details
  • Form submissions – any information you voluntarily provide via contact or quote request forms

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To respond to your enquiries and requests
  • To send marketing communications (only with your consent)
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To analyse website traffic and usage patterns via Google Analytics

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers – hosting, email, and analytics providers who assist in operating our website
  • Legal requirements – when required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business partners – only with your explicit consent

5. Data Retention

  • Contact enquiries: 12 months
  • Analytics data: 26 months
  • Marketing data: until you unsubscribe

6. Your Rights (GDPR & UK Data Protection Act 2018)

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Request data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO)

7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including SSL/TLS encryption, Content Security Policy headers, and regular security reviews to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

8. Cookies

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website. For full details, please see our Cookie Policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
